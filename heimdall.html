// MISSION

Seeing Everything
in Orbit.

Space is getting crowded. From roughly 3,000 active satellites in 2020 to an estimated 100,000 by 2030, the need to understand the orbital environment has never been greater.

Heimdall aggregates all major orbital data sources into a single, continuously updated 3D environment — from lone cubesats to mega-constellations, debris to crewed stations, LEO to GEO.

Solving space congestion is collaborative. Heimdall is our contribution.
